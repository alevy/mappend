About mappend

mappend is a weblog by amit levy (that's me!), where he writes about whatever — but primarily about programming. Amit is a PhD student in computer science and loves hearing the sound of his own voice. He also enjoys bargain hunting and non-sequitors.

The name mappend is taken from the Haskell function on Monoids (sort of a generalization of lists, but not really). Get it? It's a weblog, so you can, like, append stuff to it.

mappend is built in Haskell using the Simple web framework. The source code is available under the LGPL here
